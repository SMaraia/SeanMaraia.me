Tower Defender

I. High Concept

  • The simplest tower defense - You are the tower, defend yourself!

II. Genre

  • Tower defense/shooter

III. Platform

  • Primary Target: iOS universal
  • Secondary Target: tvOS

IV. Story

You’re a tower now.  Defend yourself.

V. Aesthetics

2D feel, 8bit-ish sprites - I can do these in-house if necessary

VI. Gameplay

Mechanics

  • Tower has health (so you don’t die in one hit)
  • Depth - Powerups
  •         Either by combos/not missing, or by actually getting the powerup (shooting it to gain it)
  •         Maybe upgrade screen between waves?
  •         Choose between defense or offense powerups
  • Defense
  • Shields
  • Slow Enemy movement
  • Offense
  • Spreadshot
  • AoE explosion

Control

  • Tap a target and the tower will rotate to and then fire at it
  • Hold down to continue firing?
  • TV: swipe left to rotate CCW, swipe right to rotate CW

Teaching the game/New user experience aka "Onboarding"

Simple controls that can be learned easily, such as tapping to shoot, should require only a label to show the user what to do, and other mechanics shouldn’t be too complex either
