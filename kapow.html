Zombie Fiesta

This is the design document for our project KAPOW!, created during the Fall section of my Game Design and Development class, as our final Sprint.The goal with this project was to push ourselves as hard as we could, and create a Turn Based Tactics game, similar to Fire Emblem, with the theme being based on Golden-Age Superhero Comics. I was responsible for conceptualizing and implemtenting the User Interface in this game, and the final product is something I am very proud of, despite being very difficult and time consuming to create.
